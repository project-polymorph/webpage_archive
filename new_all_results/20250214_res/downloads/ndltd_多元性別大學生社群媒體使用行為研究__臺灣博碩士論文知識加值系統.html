本研究之主要目的在了解多元性別大學生在社群媒體的使用行為，以計畫行為理論及生活實驗室（living lab）模式概念為主要理論基礎，並根據焦點團體訪談方式，檢測多元性別大學生的社群媒體使用行為動機、態度與社群媒體對其行為的影響。另引用已發表論文之研究使用量表，擬定本研究問卷架構。
本研究以多元性別傾向大學生為抽樣對象，透過問卷方式進行樣本蒐集，共回收有效問卷102份。採用SPSS17.0進行研究樣本資料分析。研究方法採用問卷調查法，並進行信、效度分析、典型相關、相關分析統計驗證。研究結果發現，多元性別大學生在社群媒體中的使用動機首要為著重社群認同感及人際關係的支持，傾向地域性之環境因素影響多元性別大學生選擇使用社群的行為模式。相關分析後歸納出多元性別大學生針對社群媒體使用行為的態度及特性；「社群媒體特性」：1.社群認同性2.享樂性3.人際關係性4.網站實用性，和「社群媒體行為態度」：1.態度2.主觀規範3.社群互動4.知覺行為控制5.行為意向。本研究結果顯示多元性別大學生在九項社群媒體使用行為上均達到顯著的標準。研究結果可提供相關探討多元性別族群行為及促進性/別平等教育推廣的參考。

The major purpose of this study lies in understanding the behavior in social media on college students in LGBT group. Based on living lab and theory of planned behavior with focus group method, we examed how the motivation, attitude on using social media influence the behavior of college students in LGBT group. In this study, we also applied measure scale in published thesis of master degree to making the framework of questionnaire.

The sampling objectives are college students in LGBT group. We collected some 102 samples by taking questionnaire and analyzed sample datum by SPSS version 17.0. Questionnaire investigation is taken as the methodology, and the datum are statistically tested by reliability, validity, canonical correlation analysis and correlation analysis. The major results draw that the top motivations of using social media are social identification and support of human relationship, which also make the region tend to be the factor of choosing the social media. By correlation analysis, we induced that the social behavior attitudes and social characteristics of college students in LGBT group on using social media. The social characteristics include social identification, entertainment, social relationship and website practicability. The social behavior attitudes include attitude, subjective norm, social interation, perceived behavior control and behavior intention. The result shows that college students in LGBT group reach the statistically significant level on all 9 items of using behavior on social media, which also can be the reference for studying the people belongs to multi-gender group and improve or promote LGBT education.
