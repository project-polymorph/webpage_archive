2024/5/25(Sat) "You Better Werq!" 第二屆台北國際變裝藝術節Taipei International Drag Fest Vol.2

The annual drag festival that celebrates drag, queerness and artistry!

 

🌈台灣第一人勇闖魯保羅變裝秀，台灣之光蕉后Nymphia Wind載譽歸國//第十六季人氣天后Mirage & Plane Jane//菲律賓變裝秀第二季M1ss Jade So訪台演出//台灣史上規模最大變裝盛事//網羅全台超過30位最受歡迎的變裝藝術家全員集合同台爭豔

 

Ｏn May 25th, the highly anticipated ‘You Better Werq!’ Taipei International Drag Fest Vol. 2 will be take place in Taipei at Hanaspace花漾展演空間. It’s the biggest Drag event of the year and the festival’s mission is to celebrate and promote ‘The Art of Drag’ in Taiwan, and also to provide an inclusive space for queer and trans performance artists. Among the festival’s headliners will be Nymphia Wind, who is on the current season of RuPaul’s Drag Race (Season 16) and Taiwan's first ever participant on RuPaul's Drag Race. This will be her official ‘Homecoming’ and we’re excited to announce that two of her Season 16 sisters, Mirage and Plane Jane, will also be joining her. And to complete our International lineup, the rising Drag Superstar from ‘Drag Race Philippines (S2)' - M1ss Jade So - will also be joining them to serve us some Asian Drag Excellence.

 

They will be performing alongside some of THE BEST and most popular drag artists in Taiwan. More than 30+ drag artists and performers will join forces to bring you the most spectacular and jaw dropping shows at the BIGGEST drag event in Taiwan!
