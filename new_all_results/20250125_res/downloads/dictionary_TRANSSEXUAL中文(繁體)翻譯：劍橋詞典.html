transsexual 在英語-中文（繁體）詞典中的翻譯

transsexual
adjective
uk
/trænzˈsek.ʃu.əl/ us
/trænzˈsek.ʃu.əl/
Add to word list Add to word list
used to describe a person whose gender is not the same as the physical body they were born with
（尤指男性）易性癖者
Gender transition refers to the period during which transsexual persons begin changing their appearance and bodies to match their internal identity.
同義詞
transgender
used to describe a person who has had surgery to change their body so it matches their gender
變性人
After her sex change operation, she made history by becoming the first transsexual woman to compete in the tournament.
Note:
  • The word transsexual is not as common now as it used to be, and it can be offensive to some people. Only use this word to describe someone if they use it to describe themselves.
