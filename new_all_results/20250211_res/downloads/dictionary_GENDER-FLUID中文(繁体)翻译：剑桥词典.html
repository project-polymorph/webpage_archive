gender-fluid 在英语-中文（繁体）词典中的翻译

gender-fluid
adjective
(also genderfluid)
uk
/ˌdʒen.dəˈfluː.ɪd/ us
/ˌdʒen.dɚˈfluː.ɪd/
Add to word list Add to word list
not having only one gender, or moving freely between genders
性別流動的（即主張性別認同是動態變化的，並非局限於特定的一種）
Some people identify as gender-fluid. 一些人認為自己的性別是流動性的。
This is my friend Kay, and they are genderfluid.
The concept of being gender-fluid may seem odd to some, but it's an accepted part of many cultures around the world.
更多范例减少例句
  • Gender-fluid individuals may prefer to not make further statements about their gender.
  • Genderfluid children do not abide by the binary norms of gender prescribed by the culture.
