How it works

  • Step 1

    Join & Set Your Personal Goals
    Create your account, complete the online assessment and get started.

  • Step 2

    Get A Bespoke Transition Pack
    We use a decade of unique transgender data and harness your personal goals to craft a unique Transition Pack that will support your care in every setting.

  • Step 3

    Referrals for Prescriptions & Other Services
    With your Transition Pack by your side, get referred to independent doctors and practitioners who can consult and prescribe if required.

  • Step 4

    Because Your Journey Is For Life
    Your Transition Pack evolves with you and your changing goals benefitting from cutting edge gender-affirming care standards so you can get the very best support from your healthcare network.

Experts in trans healthcare

GenderGP’s worldwide transgender services provide advocacy, support and access to a range of complementary services. We enable trans people to live their lives more easily supporting gender-affirming care with data and links to independent services. 

We make gender-affirming care, advice, and education more accessible to gender-diverse people and those who support them using data and analytics. Our long-term goal is to alleviate the suffering associated with discrimination and prejudice too often faced by the transgender community through advocacy and improving the healthcare support available to transgender people.

Frequently Asked Questions

Still have questions? Get Help Now

How can I get started with GenderGP?

Getting started is easy! Simply click here and create an account. You can then browse our services and choose the ones that are best to support your journey. We also offer a quick consultation to discuss your needs and answer any questions you may have.

How do I get reliable and qualified transgender healthcare?

GenderGP is a platform that connects you with a network of qualified professionals who specialise in transgender healthcare. We offer a variety of services, including hormone therapy, counselling, and support, to help you on your gender journey.

Does GenderGP offer reliable and qualified transgender healthcare?

We can concierge your journey from Transition Pack to independent referral to a curated network of practitioners including experienced and passionate doctors, counsellors, and therapists who are dedicated to providing high-quality, gender-affirming care.

Is GenderGP membership right for me?

Join GenderGP and stay in control of your transition! As a member you will access our 24/7 Intelligent Transition Pack. Making the world’s best medical practice and international trans care standards available and personalised for you. From prescription referrals to doctors consultations your  Transition Pack can open up your options and help improve your journey.

How do I leave a review for GenderGP?

We value your feedback! You can leave a review for GenderGP on Trustpilot,  Google, social media pages, or trusted healthcare review platforms. Your feedback helps us improve our services and ensures we’re meeting the needs of our community.
