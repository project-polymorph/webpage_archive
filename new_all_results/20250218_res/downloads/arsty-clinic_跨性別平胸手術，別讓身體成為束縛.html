▌Principles of flat chest surgery

Cup size B and below: Mastectomy combined with liposuction

Using VASER liposuction , liposuction is performed from the armpits, areola or both sides of the chest. The fat in the breast is first sucked out, and then the incision is made around the areola to remove the mammary gland and fat tissue. Depending on the situation, excess skin is removed and then sutured. The liposuction incision is minimally invasive and the scar is not obvious. The incision scar is located at the junction of the areola and the skin, and is also less obvious after recovery.

C cup and above: Mastectomy 

If the breasts are large and sagging, surgical procedures are recommended to remove fat and excess skin in a large area using the inverted T-cut method (lollipop cut), while at the same time transplanting the areola and raising the nipple to an appropriate position. The nerves will not be cut, and most breasts will retain sensitivity after the operation.

▌Does flat breast surgery need to be combined with nipple reduction/areola reduction surgery?

Generally speaking, flat chest surgery is often combined with nipple reduction and areola reduction surgery. This is because men’s areola and nipples are usually smaller. Therefore, for the sake of postoperative beauty and proportion, the nipples and areola are usually modified according to individual circumstances to make the postoperative chest look more ideal and proportional.

▌Scars and recovery period after flat chest surgery

The recovery period of flat chest surgery is about one week, and the scar will gradually fade in about 6-12 months. If you feel that it is obvious, you can also undergo pulsed light, laser and other scar lightening treatments. The recovery period is about one week. Avoid exerting force on the chest or lifting heavy objects within two weeks, avoid strenuous exercise within one month, and wear a body shaper as prescribed by the doctor to help postoperative recovery. In addition, it should be noted that flat chest surgery is "irreversible". Once the breasts and breasts are removed, you cannot breastfeed, and they cannot be reconstructed to restore their original appearance. You must consider it carefully.

▌Precautions after flat chest surgery

1. In the first two days after surgery, it is normal for the exudate to be pink. Please change the gauze to keep the wound dry. If the exudate becomes increasingly red and thick, please return for a checkup immediately.

2. You can apply ice compresses one week after the operation and are encouraged to return to daily life and work as soon as possible, which will help with recovery.

3. After the stitches are removed in the first week after the operation, cosmetic tape will be applied to reduce the redness, swelling and melanin deposition of the wound.

4. After the operation, the liposuction area can be compressed by the body shaper to achieve the desired effect. Wearing the body shaper insufficiently may cause the surgical area to heal and achieve the best effect later, and may also cause the formation of subcutaneous lumps. If subcutaneous lumps are formed, the body may need one to three months to absorb them. At this time, massage can be used to help absorption.

5. Swelling and numbness at the surgical site after the second week are normal and will gradually recover in about two to three months.

6. Follow-up visits will be scheduled one day, two days, one week, one month, three months, six months, or regularly after surgery.

7. Avoid smoking, drinking, and other irritating foods that affect the healing speed of wounds.

8. Avoid strenuous exercise and lifting heavy objects one week after the operation to avoid rupture of blood vessels in the wound.

Flat chest surgery complications and risk of failure

Generally speaking, any surgery has its risks. Detailed communication with professional doctors before surgery can effectively reduce the risks. Flat chest surgery may cause wound infection, bruises and swelling, poor nipple circulation, temporary numbness, scar hyperplasia, and breast depression. Therefore, it is very important to find a trustworthy plastic surgery clinic and doctor with professional skills. Doctors with rich clinical experience can quickly make correct judgments for surgical emergencies; and high-end advanced instruments and equipment can make the surgical process smoother and more stable.
