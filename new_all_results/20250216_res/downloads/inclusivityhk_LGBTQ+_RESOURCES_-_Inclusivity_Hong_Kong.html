LGBTQ+ RESOURCES

INCLUSIVITY HK IN-HOUSE RESOURCES

These resources were created from scratch by InclusivityHK organizers, with the explicit goal of targeting and catering to HK’s students. If you don’t see your questions answered by one of the resources listed below, we advise you to take a look further down, where we’ve listed external resources. Please note that InclusivityHK is not responsible for the content or any and all advertisements listed on external resources.

FAQs ABOUT THE LGBTQ+ COMMUNITY, ANSWERED

Have a question about the LGBTQ+ community? Odds are, you’re not alone! Check out our FAQs about the LGBTQ community, answered by InclusivityHK organizers who identify as queer!

LEARN MORE

PRONOUNS 101: A USEFUL GUIDE

Check out this informative guide created by InclusivityHK organizers to the concept of pronouns! It outlines a lot of important information in a bite-size format, including distinguishing between gender and sex, introducing pronouns, and providing a quick and comprehensive list of do’s and don’t’s for those getting acquainted with the concept of pronouns!

RESOURCES FOR MEMBERS OF THE LGBTQ+ COMMUNITY SEEKING SUPPORT

Note: Some ads on these sites may not be suitable for younger viewers. Please exercise viewer discretion.

Learn more

The Trevor Project – An online resource for LGBTQ+ youth seeking support and guidance – although the hotlines are for US use only, the website contains many useful resources for international members of the community, such as TrevorSpace, an online community for LGBTQ+ youth, and the Trevor Support Center, a resource center for LGBTQ+ youth and allies.

Transgender Resource Center / Rainbow HK

The Transgender Resource Center is an online resource center for members of the trans community in HK. Rainbow HK offers legal support, rapid HIV testing and domestic violence intervention.
