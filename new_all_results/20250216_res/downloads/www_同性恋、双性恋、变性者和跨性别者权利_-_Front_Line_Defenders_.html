#同性恋、双性恋、变性者和跨性别者权利

同性恋、双性恋、变性者和跨性别权利日益受到承认和支持，即便世界上仍有很多国家强烈试图将这些性与性别认同少数群体非法化。2015年，国际同性恋、双性恋、变性者和跨性别协会（ILGA）报道了75个国家将同性关系作为非法行为，另外也有一些国家制订了针对LGBTI群体的反“同性恋宣传”的法律。尽管如此，几乎每一个国家LGBTI群体的人权捍卫者都在积极寻求改善LGBTI群体的处境，使其人权获得尊重。

LGBT+ rights are gaining in recognition and support, even as there are strong attempts to criminalize the LGBTI community in a number of countries around the world.

In 2015, the International Lesbian, Gay, Bisexual, Trans and Intersex Association (ILGA) reported 75 countries that criminalize same-sex relationships, though others also have introduced laws against 'homosexual propaganda' aimed at targeting the LGBTI community.  Nonetheless, LGBTI rights defenders are active in virtually every country seeking to improve conditions and respect for the human rights of the LGBTI community.
