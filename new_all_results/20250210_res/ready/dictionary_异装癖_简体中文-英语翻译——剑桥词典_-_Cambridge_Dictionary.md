# 异装癖的翻译和定义

## 异装癖

**翻译:** [transvestism]  

**发音:**  
- 美式: /trænsˈves.tɪ.zəm/  
- 英式: /trænzˈves.tɪ.zəm/  

**定义:**  
The practice of wearing clothes usually associated with the opposite sex, as a form of self-expression, usually by a heterosexual man who sometimes wears women's clothes, make-up, jewellery, etc.

In addition to biographies of Shakespeare and Dickens, he has written a book on transvestism.
